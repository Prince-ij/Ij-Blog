About the Writer

Education:

He currently studies Computer Science in Ahmadu Bello University. He wishes to graduate and give back to his Country through the knowledge acquired.

How did he start writing ?:

Ibrahim Aminu started writing right from the age of nine, where he would write small stories and share with his siblings and friends. He quickly found out that the feeling that satisfied him the most was holding down his pen unto the paper and pouring down his imaginations and thoughts. He took it from there and began writing articles, though only few saw the light of the world at that time. But now Ij had started this blog with the aim of furthering this passion and taking it to the next level.

How did he get into tech ?

It was during the covid 19 pandemic, while he was waiting for admissions that the world of software piqued his interest. He started off from sololearn where he first learnt html, css and other programming languages. But Ij never tasted what is like to be a software Engineer until one lucky day he learnt about a scholarship to study Software Engineering at AlX from his coursemate "Jannah" whose name he will never forget. He applied for the course and was quickly astonished at the well designed course, he learnt a lot about not only coding but Software Engineering practices and Industry conventions, the ins and out of building applications. The most important he got from that course though was the mindset it gave him. The "DO HARD THINGS" brand stick with him as he continued to push further up to this day.
